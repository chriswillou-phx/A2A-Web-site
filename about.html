My Story

My passion for marketing began long before my career did. Growing up across Mexico, Norway, Japan, and the U.S., I became fascinated by how people’s customs, languages, and habits shape the way they see and interact with the world.

Today, marketing is more complex than ever — and more measurable than ever. Yet most organizations still struggle to translate activity into impact.

Over my 30-year career, I’ve worked in leading agencies and built high-performing in-house teams across travel, hospitality, real estate, loyalty, retail, and CPG. I help organizations transform marketing from a tactical function into an accountable driver of growth.
