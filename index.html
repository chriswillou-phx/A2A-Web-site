The Problem

Most marketing teams are busy — but not accountable. Activity is high. Budgets are spent. Campaigns launch. Yet leadership still struggles to understand what’s working, why it’s working, and how marketing contributes to business results.

What’s missing isn’t effort. It’s clarity, structure, and leadership.

How I Help

I work with organizations that need marketing leadership — not just marketing execution.

As a fractional CMO and senior marketing advisor, I help companies bring discipline and accountability to their marketing function by defining a clear marketing vision aligned to business goals, designing and scaling the right team structure, clarifying brand positioning and customer experience, and building data-driven measurement and KPI frameworks.

About

My passion for marketing began long before my career did. Having grown up across multiple countries, I developed an early fascination with human behavior — what drives decisions, builds trust, and creates connection.

Over my 30-year career, I’ve worked in leading agencies and built high-performing in-house teams across travel, hospitality, real estate, loyalty, retail, and CPG. I help organizations transform marketing from a tactical function into an accountable driver of growth.
